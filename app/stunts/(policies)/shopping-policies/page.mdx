# Shopping Policies

*Last Updated: January 8, 2025*

## 1. Customer Service

### Contact Information
We're here to help! You can reach our customer service team through any of the following methods:

- **Email**: admin@madebycommon.com
- **Business Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM PST
- **Response Time**: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days

For urgent matters, please use our contact form and mark your request as "Urgent" for priority handling.

## 2. Subscription & Billing

### 2.1 Subscription Terms
- All subscriptions are billed in advance on a recurring basis
- Available billing cycles: Monthly
- Prices are listed in USD and include applicable taxes
- Payment is processed securely through Stripe

### 2.2 Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit Cards
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay

## 3. Refund Policy

### 3.1 General Terms
- We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions
- Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of initial purchase
- Refunds will be processed to the original payment method

### 3.2 Refund Eligibility
Refunds will be considered for:
- Technical issues preventing service use
- Accidental purchases or duplicate charges
- Dissatisfaction with service during first 14 days

Refunds may not be available for:
- Subscriptions active longer than 14 days
- Usage exceeding 50% of monthly quota
- Violations of Terms of Service

### 3.3 Refund Process
1. Submit refund request to our email address
2. Include order number and reason for refund
3. Expect processing within 3-5 business days
4. Receive confirmation email once processed

## 4. Cancellation Policy

### 4.1 Subscription Cancellation
- You may cancel your subscription at any time
- Access continues until the end of your billing period
- No partial refunds for unused time
- No cancellation fees

### 4.2 How to Cancel
1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to Subscription Settings
3. Click "Manage Subscription"
4. Follow prompts to confirm cancellation
5. Receive cancellation confirmation email

### 4.3 Post-Cancellation
- Your account remains active until period end
- Projects and data remain accessible until period end
- Download any content you wish to keep
- Reactivate anytime by renewing subscription

## 5. Dispute Resolution

### 5.1 Billing Disputes
If you notice unauthorized charges or billing errors:
1. Contact customer service immediately
2. Provide transaction details and evidence
3. Allow up to 5 business days for investigation
4. Receive resolution notification via email

### 5.2 Service Disputes
For service-related issues:
1. Submit detailed description to provided email address
2. Include relevant screenshots or error messages
3. Our team will investigate and respond within 24 hours
4. Escalate to management if needed
